About Game:

I have developed a captivating VR game utilizing the Unity engine. This immersive game consists of three diverse levels, each offering unique targets and presenting players with distinct world styles. To achieve victory in each level, players must navigate the environment and locate the exit point.

To facilitate gameplay, I have incorporated an intuitive hint system. As players enter the game, pertinent hints will be prominently displayed on the screen, providing valuable guidance and enhancing their overall experience.

In summary, this professionally-crafted VR game showcases my expertise in Unity development. With its engaging levels, varying targets, captivating world styles, and the provision of helpful hints, players are sure to be captivated by the challenging yet rewarding gameplay.
