The game features an engaging gameplay mechanic where players must tap the screen to navigate through a challenging obstacle course. The objective is to avoid collision with any of the dynamically generated obstacles. In the event of a collision, the game restarts, allowing players to attempt a higher score.

As players progress through the game, an updated score is continuously displayed on the screen, providing a sense of achievement and encouraging them to improve their performance.

This Unity project combines responsive touch controls, procedurally generated obstacles, and an intuitive scoring system to deliver an immersive and enjoyable gaming experience. Players are prompted to develop their reflexes and strategize their movements, leading to engaging gameplay sessions and a competitive atmosphere.
